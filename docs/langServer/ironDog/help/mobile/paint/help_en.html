Paint Game Rules

  • Paint is a 5-reel, 3-row video slot featuring free spins and a bonus game.
  • You play a fixed 20 lines per spin (1 coin per line), the total bet value is shown on the bet button.
  • To change the bet value, open the bet panel and select one from the wheel.
  • In the BET SETTINGS menu the DISPLAY IN COINS option toggles the balance display and payout values from CASH to coins, the bet value is always shown in CASH. The value of the coin is: Bet value/number of lines.
  • The spin button plays the game at the current bet value (you can drag the reels up or down to spin if the feature is enabled in the settings menu).
  • Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the PAYTABLE.
  • A coin Line wins is equal to the value shown on the PAYTABLE.
  • Only the highest winning combination on an active bet line from leftmost to right is paid.
  • Wild symbols substitute for all non-scatter symbols.
  • Simultaneous wins on different bet lines are added.
  • 5 free spins are activated by collecting 5 of the golden symbols on the middle reel.
  • In free spins collecting a golden symbol will award an extra free spin up to a maximum of 5 extra free spins for a total of 10.
  • There are scatter symbols (one per reel) in free spins that award a prize and multiplier.
  • The Bonus game is activated by collecting 3 or more bonus symbols on a winline, which must be on consecutive reels starting from the left.
  • Game History is not available when PLAYING FOR FUN.
  • Open the AUTOPLAY menu, holding the spin button down, to adjust round numbers (click the PLAY button inside to start and eventually the STOP button to stop it at any time). To adjust stop criteria open the autoplay settings in the menu.

Advanced Autoplay Options

  • To set advanced autoplay options click the hamburger menu in the top left corner of the game and then open AUTOPLAY symbol in the menu panel.
  • Stop on any win. Stop Autoplay when you win in a round.
  • Stop on win of. Stop Autoplay when the amount you win exceeds or equals the amount you specify.
  • Stop if cash increases by. Stop Autoplay if cash increases by the amount you specify.
  • Stop if cash decreases by. Stop Autoplay if cash decreases by the amount you specify.
  • Note: When changing Autoplay settings during a game round, all settings will take effect once the game round or feature is completed.
  • Note: If you are disconnected while playing, all Autoplay settings will return to default when you reload the game.
  • Note: Some autoplay options are mandatory for some jurisdictions.

Paint free spins

  • 5 free spins are activated after collecting 5 of the golden characters on the middle reel.
  • Inside free spins collecting more golden characters will award an extra free spin.
  • There are 4 scatter characters inside free spins which award a fixed coin prize and a multiplier, they can only exit on their own reel (see PAYTABLE for details).
  • Multipliers are added together and applied at the end of the free spins to the total winnings.

Paint bonus game

  • Bonus game in paint can be activated by collecting 3 or more bonus symbols.
  • The symbols must be on adjacent reels starting from the leftmost reel to win.
  • The number of symbols you get increases the multiplier for the bonus game(see PAYTABLE for details).
  • Inside the bonus game, click a bubble to reveal a multiplier prize.
  • At the end of the bonus game, you will get your stake multiplied by the sum of prizes you found in the bubbles, multiplied again by the tier of bonus you activated.
