• Blood Queen is a 5-reel, 3-row video slot featuring wild reel during regular game, Free Spins, shifting wild reel and expanding sticky wild during the Free Spin game.
  • You play a fixed 30 lines per spin (1 coin per line), the total bet value is shown on the bet button.
  • To change the bet value, open the bet panel and select one by dragging the wheel.
  • In the BET SETTINGS menu the DISPLAY IN COINS option toggles the balance display and payout values from CASH to COINS, the bet value is always shown in CASH. The value of the coin is: Bet value/number of lines.
  • The spin button plays the game at the current bet value (you can drag the reels up or down to spin if the feature is enabled in the settings menu).
  • Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the PAY TABLE.
  • A coin line win is equal to the value shown on the PAY TABLE.
  • Only the highest winning combination on an active bet line from left to right is paid.
  • Wild symbols substitutes for all non-special symbols.
  • Free Spin symbols can appear on any position on the reels.
  • Number of awarded FS depends on the number of FS icons displayed on the reels.
  • During regular game, wild symbols appear on the top positions of any reel and expand to cover the entire reel.
  • Special expanding sticky wild symbols and shifting wild reel are activated during the Free Spin game.
  • Game History is not available when PLAYING FOR FUN.
  • Open the AUTOPLAY menu, by holding the spin button down, to adjust amount of round numbers (click the PLAY button inside to start and eventually the STOP button to stop it at any time). To adjust stop criteria open the autoplay settings in the menu.

Advanced Autoplay Options

  • To set advanced autoplay options click AUTOPLAY symbol in the game panel.
  • Stop on any win. Stops Autoplay when you win in a round.
  • Stop on win of. Stops Autoplay when the amount you win exceeds or equals the amount specified.
  • Stop if cash increases by. Stops Autoplay if cash increases by the amount specified.
  • Stop if cash decreases by. Stops Autoplay if cash decreases by the amount specified.
  • Stop when feature entered. Stops Autoplay if any game feature is triggered.
  • Note: When changing Autoplay settings during a game round, all settings will take effect once the game round or feature is completed.
  • Note: If you are disconnected while playing, all Autoplay settings will return to default when you reload the game.
  • Note: Some autoplay options are mandatory for some jurisdictions.

WILD REEL SYMBOL during regular game

  • When a gargoyle style icon appears on any top position of any reel, it will expand covering the entire reel.
  • More than 1 wild reel can appear on the reels at the same time.
  • Wild reel completes and substitutes all icons except for Free Spin symbols.

Free Spin

  • Free Spin symbols can appear in any position on the reels.
  • The number of Free Spin being awarded depends on the number of Free Spin symbols appearing on the reels.
    • 3 symbols award 20 Free Spins
    • 4 symbols award 30 Free Spins
    • 5 symbols award 50 Free Spins
  • Depending on the initial Free Spin game selection, (Van Hell or blood Queen) two different Wild symbols might be displayed during the Free Spin game:
  • Van Hell’s Holy Water expanding sticky wild
    • The Holy Water symbol appears on any position on reels 2,3,4 and activates the expanding sticky wild. The first sticky wild icon’s position is determining the direction of the wild spreading animation. After Holy Water appears it will grow each spin until it covers 4 symbols then it will vanish for the next spin. Spreading animation starts vertically and moves horizontally towards the center.
    • More than 1 sticky wild icon can appear on the reels at the same time.
    • Any active sticky wild will stop in its position if played out during last Free Spin.
    • Wild symbols substitute for all non-special symbols.
  • Blood Queen shifting wild reel
    • The Blood Potion wild icon appears on the top of reels 2,3,4 and expands covering the entire reel. Each spin, the wild reel will shift leftwards until it reaches the first reel, where it disappears.
    • More than one wild reel can be displayed at the same time.
    • Any active wild reel will stop in its position if played out during last Free Spin and wilds will not stick past the end of Free Spins
    • Wild symbols substitute for all non-special symbols.
