• Treasure of Horus is a 5-reel, 3-row video slot featuring wild, scatter and free spin game.
  • You play a fixed 20 lines per spin (1 coin per line), the total bet value is shown on the bet button.
  • To change the bet value, open the bet panel and select one by dragging the wheel.
  • In the BET SETTINGS menu the DISPLAY IN COINS option toggles the balance display and payout values from CASH to COINS, the bet value is always shown in CASH. The value of the coin is: Bet value/number of lines.
  • The spin button plays the game at the current bet value (you can drag the reels up or down to spin if the feature is enabled in the settings menu).
  • Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the PAYTABLE.
  • A coin line win is equal to the value shown on the PAYTABLE.
  • Only the highest winning combination on an active bet line from left to right is paid.
  • Scarabs may fly over or land on the reels and change some of the reel symbols into wilds or free spins.
  • Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except scatter and free spins.
  • Wild symbols can award special multipliers.
  • Simultaneous wins on different bet lines are added.
  • A scarab may drop a Free spin symbol on the top central position of the centre reel, which will expand to cover the entire reel.
  • 6 free spins are awarded when any of the Free spin symbols appear on the reels.
  • Prizes are awarded when 3+ golden mask scatter symbols appear anywhere on the reels in the Tomb of Horus.
  • Game History is not available when PLAYING FOR FUN.
  • Open the AUTOPLAY menu, by holding the spin button down, to adjust amount of round numbers (click the PLAY button inside to start and eventually the STOP button to stop it at any time). To adjust stop criteria open the autoplay settings in the menu.

Advanced Autoplay Options

  • To set advanced autoplay options click the menu icon in the top left corner of the game and then select the AUTOPLAY symbol in the menu panel.
  • Stop on any win. Stops autoplay when you win a round.
  • Stop on win of. Stops autoplay when the amount won exceeds or equals the amount specified.
  • Stop if cash increases by. Stops autoplay if cash increases by the amount specified.
  • Stop if cash decreases by. Stops autoplay if cash decreases by the amount specified.
  • Note: When changing autoplay settings during a game round, all settings will take effect once the game round or feature is completed.
  • Note: If you are disconnected while playing, all autoplay settings will return to default when you reload the game.
  • Note: Some autoplay options are mandatory for some jurisdictions.

Treasure of Horus free spins

  • A scarab may land and drop a Free spin symbol on the top central position of the central reel. The Free spin symbol expands to cover the entire reel and gives the user access to three secret chambers where big treasure is hidden.
  • The number of awarded Free spins is always 6.
  • When Free spins are activated on the reels and the user gets access to the next secret chamber level, won Free spins are added to the remaining spins from the previous chamber.
  • Getting access to secret chambers - as the user enters the deeper chambers they also get access to additional features:
  • 1st chamber:
    • Green wild (x1)
  • 2nd chamber:
    • Green wild (x1)
    • Pink wild (x2)
  • 3rd chamber:
    • Green wild (x1)
    • Pink wild (x2)
    • Blue wild (x5)
    • Golden mask scatter (additional coins)
  • Scarabs can drop Wild symbols in any position on the reels.
  • Wild symbols can award additional multipliers during Free spins.
  • Wild symbols have their own paytable.
  • If more than one wild symbol appears on the reels completing a winning combination, only the highest multiplier is applied.
  • Scatter symbols can appear in the third chamber free spin game and awards additional coins as per the paytable.

  • Autoplay Page
    • Autoplay. See Advanced Autoplay Options earlier.
  • Game History
    • Game history list. List of all the games played (not available in FUN mode), click on a row to see all the available details.
  • Bet settings page
    • Display in coins. Toggles between coins and cash, it only affects total balance and winnings, bet is always displayed in cash using the current currency.
  • Home page. Click the Home button to come back to the lobby.
