• Gifts of Ostara is a 5-reel, 3-row video slot featuring wild reel, free spins, scatter, special multiplier and re-spin symbols.
  • You play a fixed 20 lines per spin (1 coin per line), the total bet value is shown on the bet button.
  • To change the bet value, open the bet panel and select one by dragging the wheel.
  • In the BET SETTINGS menu the DISPLAY IN COINS option toggles the balance display and payout values from CASH to COINS, the bet value is always shown in CASH. The value of the coin is: Bet value/number of lines.
  • The spin button plays the game at the current bet value (you can drag the reels up or down to spin if the feature is enabled in the settings menu).
  • Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the PAYTABLE.
  • A coin line win is equal to the value shown on the PAYTABLE.
  • Only the highest winning combination on an active bet line from leftmost to right is paid.
  • A golden egg special symbol can pop out during regular game revealing special prizes: scatter, multiplier, wild reel, sticky wild reel, re-spin and up to 5 special symbols.
  • A 30x multiplier is awarded when a scatter prize is revealed.
  • A x2, x4, x6, x8 or x10 multiplier is awarded when a game multiplier is revealed.
  • Wild reel substitutes for all non-special symbols and is awarded when a wild reel symbol is revealed.
  • 3 free spins are awarded when a sticky wild reel symbol is revealed.
  • A free single re-spin is awarded when a silver re-spin bunny is revealed.
  • 10 free spins are activated when 3 special symbols in a row of the same type are being collected.
  • Game History is not available when PLAYING FOR FUN.
  • Open the AUTOPLAY menu, by holding the spin button down, to adjust amount of round numbers (click the PLAY button inside to start and eventually the STOP button to stop it at any time). To adjust stop criteria open the autoplay settings in the menu.

Advanced Autoplay Options

  • To set advanced autoplay options click the menu icon in the top left corner of the game and then select the AUTOPLAY symbol in the menu panel.
  • Stop on any win. Stops Autoplay when you win a round.
  • Stop on win of. Stops Autoplay when the amount you win exceeds or equals the amount specified.
  • Stop if cash increases by. Stops Autoplay if cash increases by the amount specified.
  • Stop if cash decreases by. Stops Autoplay if cash decreases by the amount specified.
  • Note: When changing Autoplay settings during a game round, all settings will take effect once the game round or feature is completed.
  • Note: If you are disconnected while playing, all Autoplay settings will return to default when you reload the game.
  • Note: Some autoplay options are mandatory for some jurisdictions.

Golden egg special symbol

  • A golden egg special symbol can pop out from the hole in the ground during regular game revealing special prizes: scatter, multiplier, wild reel, sticky wild reel, re-spin and up to 5 special symbols.
  • SCATTER
  • A 30x multiplier is awarded and applied to the current bet when a golden bunny is revealed.
  • MULTIPLIER
  • A x2, x4, x6, x8 or x10 multiplier is awarded and applied to the current spin when a game multiplier prize is revealed.
  • WILD REEL
  • When a wild reel prize is revealed, a wild reel appears on the reels and substitutes for all non-special symbols of the current spin.
  • STICKY WILD REEL
  • When a sticky wild reel prize is revealed, 3 free spins are awarded. During the FS game, a wild reel appears and freezes on the reels substituting for all non-special symbols.
  • RE-SPIN
  • One free single re-spin is awarded when a re-spin silver bunny is revealed.
  • COLORED EGG SPECIAL SYMBOLS
  • 10 free spins are activated when 3 colored egg symbols in a row of the same type are being revealed and collected in the side counter.
  • The colored egg which triggered the FS game will award special multiplier any time it appears on the reels during the FS game.
    • Symbol 10: awarded multiplier x1
    • Symbol J: awarded multiplier x2
    • Symbol Q: awarded multiplier x3
    • Symbol K: awarded multiplier x4
    • Symbol A: awarded multiplier x5
  • At the end of the game, the total number of special symbols being collected during FS will be multiplied by the special symbol multiplier and by the initial spin bet.
