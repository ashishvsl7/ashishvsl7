• Treasure of Horus is a 5-reel, 3-row video slot featuring wild, scatter and free spin game.
  • You play a fixed 20 lines per spin (1 coin per line), the total bet value is shown on the bet button.
  • To change the bet value, open the bet panel and select one using the arrows.
  • MAX BET loads the maximum bet value.
  • COINS/CASH toggles the balance display and payout values from CASH to COINS, the bet value is always shown in CASH. The value of the coin is: Bet value/number of lines.
  • The spin button plays the game at the current bet value (you can press the spacebar if enabled in settings).
  • Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the PAYTABLE.
  • A coin line win is equal to the value shown on the PAYTABLE.
  • Only the highest winning combination on an active bet line from left to right is paid.
  • Wild symbols substitute for all non-special symbols.
  • Wild symbols award special multipliers.
  • Simultaneous wins on different bet lines are added.
  • A special Free spin symbol can appear on the top central position of the centre reel, which will expand to cover the entire reel.
  • 6 free spins are activated when the Free spin symbol appear on the reels.
  • 100 coins are awarded when 3 gloden mask scatter symbols appear on the reels.
  • Game History is not available when PLAYING FOR FUN.
  • Open the AUTOPLAY menu to adjust the amount of round numbers and stop criteria (click the PLAY button to start and eventually the STOP button to stop it at any time).

Advanced Autoplay Options

  • To set advanced autoplay options click the AUTOPLAY symbol in the game panel.
  • Stop on any win. Stops autoplay when you win a round.
  • Stop on win of. Stops autoplay when the amount won exceeds or equals the amount specified.
  • Stop if cash increases by. Stops autoplay if cash increases by the amount specified.
  • Stop if cash decreases by. Stops autoplay if cash decreases by the amount specified.
  • Note: When changing autoplay settings during a game round, all settings will take effect once the game round or feature is completed.
  • Note: If you are disconnected while playing, all autoplay settings will return to default when you reload the game.
  • Note: Some autoplay options are mandatory for some jurisdictions.

Treasure of Horus free spins

  • Free spin symbols appear on the top central position of the central reel. The Free spin symbols expands to cover the entire reel and give the user access to three secret chambers where the big treasure is hidden.
  • The number of awarded Free spins is always 6.
  • When Frees spins are activated on the reels and the user gets access to the next secret chamber level, won Free spins are getting added to the remaining ones from the previous chamber.
  • Getting access to secret chambers, the user gets also access to additional features:
  • 1st chamber:
    • Green wild (x1)
  • 2nd chamber:
    • Green wild (x1)
    • Pink wild (x2)
  • 3rd chamber:
    • Green wild (x1)
    • Pink wild (x2)
    • Blue wild (x5)
    • Golden mask scatter (additional coins)
  • Wild symbols can appear in any position on the reels during the free spin game.
  • Wild symbols can award additional multipliers during Free spin.
  • Wild symbols have their own paytable.
  • When more than one wild symbol appears on the reels completing a winning combination, the higher multiplier is applied.
  • Scatter symbol can appear in the third chamber free spin game and awards additional coins.

  • Settings page

  • Game History
