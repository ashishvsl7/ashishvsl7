Paint Game Rules

  • Paint is a 5-reel, 3-row video slot featuring free spins and a bonus game.
  • You play a fixed 20 lines per spin (1 coin per line), the total bet value is shown on the bet button.
  • To change the bet value, open the bet panel and adjust using the arrows.
  • MAX BET loads the maximum bet value.
  • COINS/CASH toggles the balance display and payout values from CASH to COINS, the bet value is always shown in CASH. The value of a coin is: Bet value/number of lines.
  • The spin button plays the game at the current bet value (you can press the spacebar if enabled in settings).
  • Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the PAYTABLE.
  • A coin line win is equal to the value shown on the PAYTABLE.
  • Only the highest winning combination on an active bet line from left to right is paid.
  • Wild symbols substitute for all non-scatter symbols.
  • Simultaneous wins on different bet lines are added.
  • 5 free spins are activated by collecting 5 of the golden symbols on the middle reel.
  • During free spins collecting a golden symbol will award an extra free spin up to a maximum of 5 extra free spins for a total of 10.
  • There are scatter symbols (one per reel) in free spins that award a prize and multiplier.
  • The bonus game is activated by collecting 3 or more bonus symbols on a winline, which must be on consecutive reels starting from the left.
  • Game History is not available when PLAYING FOR FUN.
  • Open the AUTOPLAY menu to adjust amount of rounds and stop criteria (click the PLAY button to start and the STOP button to stop at any time).

Advanced Autoplay Options

  • To set advanced autoplay options click the AUTOPLAY symbol in the game panel.
  • Stop on any win. Stops autoplay when any win is made on a round.
  • Stop on win of. Stops autoplay when the amount won exceeds or equals the amount specified.
  • Stop if cash increases by. Stops autoplay if cash increases by the amount specified.
  • Stop if cash decreases by. Stops autoplay if cash decreases by the amount specified.
  • Stop when entering any feature. Stops autoplay if a feature is entered into, ie a bonus round or free spins.
  • Note: When changing autoplay settings during a round, all settings will take effect once the game round or feature has completed.
  • Note: If you are disconnected while playing, all autoplay settings will return to default when the game is reloaded.
  • Note: Some autoplay options are mandatory for some jurisdictions.

Paint Free Spins

  • 5 free spins are activated after collecting 5 of the golden symbols on the centre reel.
  • While free spins are active collecting more golden symbols will award an additional free spin.
  • There are 4 different scatter symbols available during free spins each awards an individual fixed prize and multiplier, they can only appear on their own reel (see PAYTABLE for details).
  • Multipliers are added together and applied at the end of the free spins to the total winnings.
  • Note: Bonus symbols will not appear during free spins.
  • Note: Free spin symbols collected are stake dependent, meaning that symbols collected on one stake will not be carried over when changing to a different bet value. They will be remembered and still be present when changing back to a stake that has already been used to collect free spin symbols.

Paint Bonus Game

  • The bonus game in paint can be activated by collecting 3 or more bonus symbols.
  • The symbols must be on consecutive reels starting from the left reel to win.
  • The number of bonus symbols collected increases the multiplier for the bonus game (see PAYTABLE for details).
  • During the bonus game, click bubbles to reveal multiplier prizes.
  • At the end of the bonus game, the winnings will be - the stake multiplied by the sum of prizes found in the bubbles, multiplied again by the bonus symbol multiplier activating the round.
