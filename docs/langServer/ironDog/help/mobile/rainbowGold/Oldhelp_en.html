• Rainbow Wilds is a 5-reel, 4-row video slot with 1024 win ways, featuring wilds, wild reels, sticky wilds and tumbling during the regular game and Free Spins with incremental multipliers.
  • You play a fixed 50 coins per spin, the total bet value is shown on the bet button.
  • To change the bet value, open the bet panel and select one using the arrows.
  • MAX BET loads the maximum bet value.
  • COINS/CASH toggles the balance display and payout values from CASH to COINS, the bet value is always shown in CASH. The value of the coin is: Bet value/number of lines.
  • The spin button plays the game at the current bet value.
  • Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the PAYTABLE.
  • A coin line win is equal to the value shown on the PAYTABLE.
  • Only the highest winning combination per symbol from left to right is paid.
  • Wild symbols substitutes for all symbols.
  • Free Spins are awarded upon 4 or more successive tumbles.
  • Number of awarded FS depends on the number of successive tumbles.
  • During regular game, wild and expanding wild symbols my appear on the 2nd, 3rd, 4th and 5th reels. A sticky wild symbol may appear on the central reel.
  • Incremental multipliers are activated during the Free Spin game.
  • Game History is not available when PLAYING FOR FUN.
  • Open the AUTOPLAY menu, by holding the spin button down, to adjust amount of round numbers (click the PLAY button inside to start and eventually the STOP button to stop it at any time). To adjust stop criteria open the autoplay settings in the menu.
  • The game is subject to a payout limit of the stake multiplied by 6000 per game round. If the payout exceeds this amount for any reason then the payout will be reduced to this amount.

Advanced Autoplay Options

  • To set advanced autoplay options click AUTOPLAY symbol in the game panel.
  • Stop on any win. Stops Autoplay when you win in a round.
  • Stop on win of. Stops Autoplay when the amount you win exceeds or equals the amount specified.
  • Stop if cash increases by. Stops Autoplay if cash increases by the amount specified.
  • Stop if cash decreases by. Stops Autoplay if cash decreases by the amount specified.
  • Stop when feature entered. Stops Autoplay if any game feature is triggered.
  • Note: When changing Autoplay settings during a game round, all settings will take effect once the game round or feature is completed.
  • Note: If you are disconnected while playing, all Autoplay settings will return to default when you reload the game.
  • Note: Some autoplay options are mandatory for some jurisdictions.

WILD REEL SYMBOL during regular game

  • When a wild reel style icon appears on any top position of any reel, it will expand covering the entire reel.
  • More than 1 wild reel can appear on the reels at the same time.
  • Wild reel completes and substitutes all icons and appears on all reels except the 1st.

Free Spin

  • Free Spins are awarded by successive tumbles.
  • The number of Free Spin being awarded and the starting multiplier depends on the number of Free Spin symbols appearing on the reels.
    • 4 tumbles award 14 Free Spins and no extra starting multiplier
    • 5 tumbles award 16 Free Spins and no extra starting multiplier
    • 6 tumbles award 16 Free Spins and a 3x starting multiplier
    • 7 tumbles award 20 Free Spins and a 3x starting multiplier
    • 8 tumbles award 20 Free Spins and a 6x starting multiplier
