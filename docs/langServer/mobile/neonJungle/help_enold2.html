• Neon Jungle is a 5-reel, 3-row video slot featuring wild symbols, free spins, scatter symbols, x4 multipliers and the keno light bonus game.
  • Multiple feature icons consecutively will not trigger the feature itself.
  • The scatter prize awarded is always 250 coins, regardless of the number of scatter symbols on the reels.
  • The number of free spins awarded is always 6, regardless of the number of Free Spin symbols on the reels.
  • The multiplier feature (appearing around a symbol in a winning combination) always awards a x4 multiplier, this will apply to all active winlines using this symbol.
  • You play a fixed 20 lines per spin (1 coin per line), the total bet value is shown on the bet button.
  • To change the bet value, open the bet panel and select one from the wheel.
  • In the BET SETTINGS menu the DISPLAY IN COINS option toggles the balance display and payout values from CASH to coins, the bet value is always shown in CASH. The value of the coin is: Bet value/number of lines.
  • The spin button plays the game at the current bet value (you can drag the reels up or down to spin if the feature is enabled in the settings menu).
  • Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the PAYTABLE.
  • A coin line win is equal to the value shown on the PAYTABLE.
  • Only the highest winning combination on an active bet line from left to right is paid.
  • All game features will be triggered by the 'Neon Glow' appearing around the symbols.
  • Wild symbol substitutes for all non-special symbols.
  • Free spins are awarded when a free spin symbol, appearing in any position on the reels, is surrounded by the 'Neon Glow'.
  • The Keno light bonus game will be triggered when a Bonus symbol, appearing in any position on the reels, is surrounded by the 'Neon Glow'.
  • During the Keno light bonus game, matching the drawn numbers will be paid out according to a dedicated paytable.
  • Game History is not available when PLAYING FOR FUN.
  • Open the AUTOPLAY menu, holding the spin button down, to adjust round numbers and stop criteria (click the PLAY button to start and eventually the STOP button to stop at any time). To adjust stop criteria open the autoplay settings in the menu.

Advanced Autoplay Options

  • To set advanced autoplay options click the menu icon in the top left corner of the game and then select the AUTOPLAY symbol in the menu panel.
  • Stop on any win. Stops Autoplay when you win in any round.
  • Stop on win of. Stops Autoplay when the amount you win exceeds or equals the amount specified.
  • Stop if cash increases by. Stops Autoplay if cash increases by the amount specified.
  • Stop if cash decreases by. Stops Autoplay if cash decreases by the amount specified.
  • Note: When changing Autoplay settings during a game round, all settings will take effect once the game round or feature is completed.
  • Note: If you are disconnected while playing, all Autoplay settings will return to default when you reload the game.
  • Note: Some autoplay options are mandatory for some jurisdictions.

SCATTER SYMBOL

  • 'Neon Glow' appearing around a scatter symbol, in any position on the reels, awards 250 coins.
  • Only the highlighted scatter symbol will award coins.

X4 MULTIPLIER

  • 'Neon Glow' appearing around a symbol, in a winning combination, awards a x4 multiplier. This will apply to all active winlines using this symbol.

WILD SYMBOL

  • Wild symbols can complete winning combinations and can substitute for all non-special symbols.

FREE-SPINS

  • 'Neon Glow' appearing around a Free spin symbol, in any position on the reels, awards 6 free spins.
  • Only the highlighted Free Spin symbol will award free spins.

BONUS

  • 'Neon Glow' appearing around a Bonus symbol, in any position on the reels, activates the Keno light bonus game.
  • Each Keno draw will consist of 15 numbers from the 28 available.
  • The amount awarded will depend on how many of the drawn numbers match the numbers selected, according to the following paytable:
