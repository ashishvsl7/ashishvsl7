• Cosmic CrystalsTM is a 5-reel, 3-row video slot featuring free re-spins and sticky wild reel on non-winning spins.
  • You play a fixed 50 win lines per spin (1 coin per line), the total bet value is shown on the bet button.
  • To change the bet value, open the bet panel and select by dragging the wheel.
  • In the BET SETTINGS menu the DISPLAY IN COINS option toggles the balance display and payout values from CASH to coins and back again, the bet value is always shown in CASH. The value of the coin is: Bet value divided by number of lines.
  • The spin button plays the game at the current bet value (you can drag the reels to spin if this feature is enabled in the settings menu).
  • Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the PAY TABLE.
  • A coin line win is equal to the value shown on the PAY TABLE.
  • Only the highest winning combination on an active bet line from left to right is paid.
  • Wild symbols expand vertically and only appear on the middle three reels. They can substitute for all other symbols.
  • Simultaneous wins on different bet lines are added together.
  • Game History is not available when PLAYING FOR FUN.
  • Open the AUTOPLAY menu, by holding the spin button down, to adjust the number of rounds (click the PLAY button to start and the STOP button to stop autoplay at any time). To adjust stop criteria for autoplay open the advanced autoplay settings in the menu.

Cosmic CrystalsTM re-spin

  • Cosmic CrystalsTM features an automatic FREE re-spin after every non-winning spin.
  • There are no limitations on the number of sequential free re-spins, they will automatically re-spin until the first winning hand spin takes occurs.
  • Winning hand means the minimum amount of coins the game can pay in a single payline (see PAY TABLE).
  • Re-spins are played at the same bet as the round activating the re-spin.

Cosmic CrystalsTM Wild Reel

  • The Cosmic CrystalsTM Wild symbols can only appear on reels 2, 3 and 4.
  • One or more Cosmic CrystalsTM Wild symbols appearing on reels 2, 3, or 4 activates the Cosmic CrystalsTM Wild Reel feature.
  • During the Cosmic CrystalsTM Wild Reel feature, the Cosmic CrystalsTM Wild symbol expands vertically to cover the entire reel.
  • If the spin is a non-winning spin the Cosmic CrystalsTM Wild Reel will remain in place while the other reels re-spin (sticky Wild Reel).
  • If a new Cosmic CrystalsTM Wild symbol appears during a re-spin, it too expands vertically. If no win takes place again, it holds in place together with any previously expanded Cosmic CrystalsTM Wild Reel symbols for another re-spin, until any winning hand takes place.
  • The Cosmic CrystalsTM Wild Reel feature ends when a winning hand takes place.
